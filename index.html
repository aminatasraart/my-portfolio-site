I am Amina Tasra, a visual artist
Welcome to my creative journey

About Me

As a Moroccan woman living in the US, art has always been my way of bridging two worlds—my heritage and the experiences I’ve gathered along the way. My passion for art is something that has grown with me, deeply rooted in the vibrant culture of Morocco. I believe that art transcends words, allowing me to express emotions, memories, and stories that are often difficult to convey otherwise.

At 15, I had the honor of participating in the "Óyeme con los ojos" exhibition in Madrid in 2015, an experience that solidified my love for visual storytelling. Two years later, I was awarded the Best Artist Award at the prestigious "Le Salon du Cheval d'El Jadida" in Morocco. These experiences have fueled my drive to continue creating art that resonates deeply with others.

My Work

Echoes of the Atlas Mountains

This piece, titled "Echoes of the Mountains," was exhibited at the "Óyeme con los ojos" exhibition in 2015 and captures the serene beauty of a rural landscape, blending rich earth tones and cool blues to evoke the peaceful connection between land and sky.

Autumn Reverie

Exhibited also at the "Óyeme con los ojos" exhibition in 2015, this vibrant piece titled "Autumn Reverie" uses bold, contrasting colors to depict the dynamic interplay of light and shadow in a hilly landscape, evoking the warmth and vibrancy of an autumn day.

Flowing Through the Shadows

This artwork was also featured in the "Óyeme con los ojos" exhibition in 2015. It captures the contrast between bright autumn hues and deep, shadowed ravines, highlighting the dynamic movement of a river cutting through the landscape.

Majestic Grace

Awarded 1st place at the "Le Salon du Cheval d'El Jadida" in 2017, "Majestic Grace" captures the ethereal beauty and strength of a white horse, with soft brushstrokes and delicate shades of blue and purple that evoke a sense of movement and elegance, reflecting the spirit of this noble creature.

Solitude at Sea

"Solitude at Sea" depicts a lone boat gently anchored in calm waters, surrounded by tranquil shades of blue that evoke a sense of quiet reflection and serenity, capturing the stillness of a peaceful moment at sea.

Whispers of Dusk

"Whispers of Dusk" captures the quiet beauty of the sky transitioning from day to night, with delicate hues of pink, yellow, and blue blending together. The silhouetted power lines and trees add a sense of calm and stillness as birds glide across the peaceful evening sky.
